Comparative Symbols

Here is the meaning and examples of comparison symbols.
Click the Start button to begin if you are ready!

>: The number on the Left is larger than the number on the right.
Example: 5>2, 12>7, 18>11

<: The number on the Right is larger than the number on the left.
Example: 4<9, 8<15, 16<18

=: When number is Equal to another.
Example: 2=2, 5=5, 8=8
